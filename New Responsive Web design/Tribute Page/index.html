Here is a timeline of Dr. Peterson's life

  • 1962: Born in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada
  • 1980s: Earns a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and a Bachelor of Education from the University of Alberta.
  • 1991: Receives a PhD in Clinical Psychology from McGill University.
  • 1992: Begins teaching at the University of Toronto as an assistant professor in the Department of Psychology.
  • 1999: Publishes his first book, "Maps of Meaning: The Architecture of Belief".
  • 2016: Publishes his second book, "12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos". The book becomes a bestseller and brings Dr. Peterson international recognition.
  • 2016-2018: Becomes a prominent figure in the intellectual dark web and a target of criticism for his views on political correctness, gender identity, and freedom of speech.
  • 2019: Launches the Jordan B Peterson Podcast and the Self Authoring Suite, online self-improvement programs.
  • 2020: Continues to tour and speak internationally, and launches the Beyond Order: 12 More Rules for Life tour.
  • 2021: Announces that he is taking a break from public speaking due to health reasons.
  • 2022: Comes back to public speaking after his health recovered.
